My Story

Where it all started

I've always been fascinated by ideas — how they form, how they spread, and how they change the world. Writing became my way of processing and sharing those ideas. This blog is the result of that obsession.

I write about technology and its impact on society, the creative process, philosophy, and anything else that captures my attention. My goal is always clarity: taking complex ideas and making them accessible without losing their depth.
